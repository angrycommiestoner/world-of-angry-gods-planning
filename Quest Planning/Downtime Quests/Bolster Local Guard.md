Bolster Local Guard
“Do YOU wanna make a difference in your community?” The faded poster reads “Then volunteer for the town guard TODAY!”

**Length of Quest:** 1 Day

**Associated Skills:** Craft (Alchemy, Firearms, Leatherworking, Metalworking), Handle Animal, Heal, Intimidate, Knowledge (Engineering), Profession (Soldier, Mercenary, etc.), Ride

**Rate of Failure:** 0%

**Consequences of Failure:** Your performance is particularly unsatisfactory. You lose 1/2/3* points of fame in the region you are performing this downtime quest in. If this would cause you to have negative fame in the region, you are formally charged with a crime by the local government. Tag the Dungeon Master role.

**Associated Skill Check DCs:** DC 18 Easy; DC 25 Medium; DC 41 Hard

**Quest Repeatability:** Yes; **Skill Check DC Increase:** +2

**Rewards** +1/+2/+3* Fame in the region you completed this quest in; Scroll of Shield of Faith +1/+2/+3*; 200 GP x Character Level; 
(* : Easy/Medium/Hard)
