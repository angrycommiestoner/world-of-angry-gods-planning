[[Oleg|Oleg’s]] wife, Svetlana, is a kind and cautious individual. Raised as a farmer in rural Restov, she and Oleg moved to the city when Oleg was conscripted into the military. During his time as a guardsman and soldier, they lived meagerly, and she often hunted small game in the surrounding forests while Oleg was away, tanning pelts and preserving the meat to sell or use. 

When Oleg’s military service at last came to an end, however, the fruits of their years of meager living were made manifest. Oleg submitted all of the necessary paperwork and was permitted by the state of Restov to create their little trading post on the border of the Greenbelt. Svetlana and her husband have spent the last several years enjoying their retirement in peace.
