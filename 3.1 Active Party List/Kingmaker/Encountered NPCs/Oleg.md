Titular Proprietor of Oleg’s Trading Post, Oleg is a long-time resident of the lands just outside the Greenbelt. He runs his trading post with his wife, [[Svetlana]]. Though they have had many difficult months since the [[Stag Lord]] rose to power, the party’s protection against [[Happs Bydon]] and his crew has alleviated some of their burden, and their exploits in the Greenbelt have brought another of his fears to life. A new spattering of faces sticking their noses into Oleg and his wife’s quiet slice of rural peace have arrived at the Trading Post in recent weeks, including [[Jhod Khaven]], the Erastilean priest the party aided in recovering the lost [[Temple of the Elk]], as well as the mercenary-knight [[Kesten Garess]], who seeks capable adventurers to aid him in taking revenge upon a former mercenary companion turned to banditry after a job gone wrong, as well as lesser adventurers for hire seeking glory and fame in the unexplored regions of the Greenbelt, and even a few craftsmen and merchants, some of whom have been so audacious as to even set up tents and hawk their wares right outside of his walls!
