Happs was a crude and foul-mouthed man who turned to banditry after he was caught running a protection racket in Restov while also, in theory, serving as a soldier and city guard.

He fled the city when he learned that the law was coming for him, abandoning a wife and two children to suffer the shame of his crimes. He’s found that banditry agrees with him—he quite enjoys the outdoors, and the occasional tumble with Kressle is nice too. He suspected that she’s working for an even more important bandit, but for now is
content being the second-biggest fish in the pond.

> [!INFO] Dead Bandit Walking!
> Happs died at the hands of the Party when attempting to extort [[Oleg]] and [[Svetlana]] in the opening act of the Adventure Path.
