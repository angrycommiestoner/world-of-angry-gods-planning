## The Grand Foyer
Purple-and-emerald flames twinkle in the dim light of this entryway, gleaming in brass lanterns, which cast playful shadows about the foyer, seemingly ignoring the uncounted centuries of disuse to which they had been put. Sitting in the center of this room is a desk, stacked high with paperwork that seemed to sit in scattered, yet to the trained eye distinctly organized piles across the surface of the desk. Towards the back of the hall are a few smaller chairs and sitting tables, as well as a few doors and a wide hall leading northward.

A waifish, blue-skinned woman sits at the desk, seemingly reading over a document, though a DC 30 Perception check reveals that her glowing blue eyes are gliding, unseeing, over the same line of the page.
