# Malphas, the Turnfeather (DND 3.5 Vestige port to Pact Maker) 
## Summoning Rules
- Spirit Level: 2nd 
- Alignment: Chaotic Neutral (CN) 
- Constellation: Skull
- Binding DC: 18
## Totems
You gain a +2 totem bonus on binding checks made with Malphas if one of the following requirements is met, or a +4 bonus if all requirements are met:

- You are an elf (or of elven blood) or possess 3 ranks in Stealth or Craft (Alchemy) skills.
- You offer a black feather or a white dove's feather as part of the ceremony.
- You drink a dose of poison you have crafted yourself. The effects of the poison end once the ceremony is completed. 

## Ceremony 
You light a single black candle and scatter white feathers around the seal while whispering promises of affection and betrayal.

## Manifestation
Malphas begins his manifestation with a furious fluttering of white doves. The creatures explode out of thin air, then fly away from each other and fade from view, revealing a handsome male elf clad in black. Malphas has pale skin, black eyes, and black feathers for hair. His smile reveals black teeth, and when he speaks, his black tongue licks the air like a snake's. Malphas wears a noble's finery in funerary black, and a cloak made of raven heads and feathers hangs from his shoulders. The heads start up a raucous cry whenever he moves too much, so he remains largely still, making only small gestures with his black-gloved hands. Malphas's hoarse voice croaks and cracks when he speaks, a quality that annoys him greatly.

## Major Granted Abilities
Invisibility (Sp) As a full-round action, you can become invisible (as invisibility). The effect lasts a number of rounds equal to your binder level. Attacking ends the effect normally. At binder level 5th, you can activate this as a standard action instead. At binder level 10th, as a move action. At binder level 15th, as a swift action.

Once the invisibility ends (voluntarily or otherwise), you cannot use this ability again for 5 rounds.

Capstone Empowerment: When using invisibility, the Binder is also invisible to beings that possess the Scent ability. 

## Minor Granted Abilities
Poison Use (Ex) You are not at risk of poisoning yourself when handling poison or applying it to a weapon. In addition, poisons you apply linger on your weapons longer (they remain potent for 1 additional successful attack before degrading).

### Bird's Eye Viewing (Su) 
At will, you can summon a dove or raven (use raven statistics in either case) to aid your powers of observation. The bird appears perched on your shoulder. You have complete control over its actions, and you can see and hear what it sees and hears.

Use the bird's skill checks for its actions and observations (e.g., Perception, Stealth), but use your own skill bonuses to interpret the information it provides (e.g., Knowledge, Sense Motive). If you have trap finding, you can direct the bird to search for traps, so long as it doesn't require manipulating objects. The bird cannot speak.

You can have only one bird summoned at a time. It remains until you summon a new one, dismiss it (a standard action), it is slain, or your pact with Malphas ends.

### Sudden Strike (Ex) 
Whenever you make a melee attack (or ranged attack within 30 ft.) against a target denied its Dexterity bonus to AC (such as when flanked or flat-footed), you deal extra precision damage of 1d6 + 1d6 per four binder levels (maximum +5d6 at 20th level). This damage stacks with sneak attack (or similar precision damage) whenever both would apply to the same target. This damage is not multiplied on a critical hit. 

## Signs and Influence

**Physical Sign:** Your teeth and tongue turn black. When you activate one of Malphas’s granted abilities, black feathers briefly flutter around you before vanishing.
**Personality Influence:** While under the influence of Malphas, you fall in love too easily. A kind word or a friendly gesture can cause you to devote yourself entirely to another person. Should that person reject your affection, your broken heart mends the moment another attractive person shows you some kindness. In addition, if you have access to poison, Malphas requires that you employ it against your foes at every opportunity.
**Favored Ally:** Elf (any) and beings of a Chaotic alignment
**Favored Enemy:** Any Lawful
## Vestigial Bond (Boon):
While bound to Malphas, your summoned bird shares the abilities of a wizard’s familiar.  The binder level is equivalent to a wizard level to determine the familiar’s abilities. This vestigial bond replaces Sudden Strike.`
