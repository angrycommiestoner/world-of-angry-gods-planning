This server utilizes a blacklist of Spheres content that is not allowed under *any* conditions, in addition to a greylist, which is a list of content that is not allowed under *specific* conditions. This list will be updated and changed as we further curate the play experience that we as staff aim to provide for our players and community.

Blacklisted abilities are often but not always simply too powerful for our play environment, usually due to poorly written or ill-conceived rules text, or deliberate intent by the author to be OP.

Conditions for which a Talent, Archetype, Feat, or other ability may fall onto the greylist include, but are not limited to:

- Banned only when used in conjunction with other abilities
- Limited by custom restrictions or changes to an otherwise problematic talent
- A Talent that is perfectly acceptable when used responsibly, but that *can* be abused. These are the *most* strictly greylisted talents, and should never be requested on a player’s first PC.
- A talent from a 4th party source that has not been adequately reviewed by staff. This category may be slow to change unless content in it is requested for approval.

Each ability listed on the Blacklist and Greylist will provide a reason for the option’s placement on the list.

If an option is not on this list, that does not mean it will be automatically approved. If you find a combination that is not on this list that you feel should be, please bring it to the moderator’s attention.
